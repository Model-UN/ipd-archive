The Russian-Ukrainian war has been an ongoing crisis since February of 2022, with over 350,000 casualties, which the People’s Republic of China deems purely unacceptable. Today, the war is still active and many countries, such as the U.S., have put major sanctions on the Russian Federation.\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The representative of the People’s Republic of China to the Security Council believes that \u201cnow is not the time for symbolic grandstanding\u201d and they need to put out real resolutions to end the war. It is not a time to add \u201cfuel to the fire.\u201d\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The People’s Republic of China urges the stance of the Security Council that the war is not a matter to pull political affairs into.
