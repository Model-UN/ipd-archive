“Not that long ago, the Indian communications minster threatened me at a press conference. He said to me: ‘Do you want war? Do you want war? Minister you have your war,'” the representative from China said. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

“China’s military position is strong. Our offense and defense are firm and unwavering. Despite this strong position and our previous experience with radical Indian cabinet, China will once again graciously offer the diplomatic solution.” <\/p>\n\n\n\n

“Firstly, an immediate removal of all Indian troops from rightfully Chinese territory,” he continued. “Second, economic reparations for all the damages India has caused China. Third, India’s support for China becoming a full member of the United Nation’s General Assembly. Fourth, India returning all seized territory that are rightfully Chinese.
