In recent updates there have been news of Indian guerrilla fighters at the border with China in response to the growing India and China border conflict. Guerrilla fighters may include armed civilians and non-professional paramilitary groups, even children.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Although the council claimed that safety of their people was the biggest concern, these fighters at the high risk Indian border raise many concerns about this.
