In a brief press release with the Cabinet of the Peoples Republic of China, they have made a statement regarding Dalai Lama;<\/p>\n\n\n\n

“The disappearance of the Dalai Lama is concerning and China believes that the people will who are responsible must be held accountable. When The Dalai Lama was living in China, he was safe and sound, protected by The People\u2019s Republic of China. After leaving China, he disappeared. China finds this very concerning. China is not in any way involved with the disappearance and will work with the rest of the international community to investigate the issue.\u201d

Could there be an underlying message behind this statement? Only time will tell as this matter further advances. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
